Questions
Will students know they're talking to a bot? +
The system is transparent — it presents itself as an enquiry assistant. Students care about getting fast, accurate answers, not whether it's human. Response quality is high enough that it doesn't matter.
How do you train it on my specific academy? +
After payment, I collect your course details, fee structure, batch timings, results, scholarship schemes, and any specific FAQs. The system is configured within 48 hours and you review it before it goes live.
What if my fees or batches change? +
Updates are included in the monthly retainer. Send me the change via WhatsApp — updated within 24 hours. No extra charge.
Where do the lead details go? +
Every lead captured goes directly to you — via WhatsApp notification, email, or a monthly report, depending on your plan. You own all your student data entirely.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. No long-term lock-in. The setup fee is a one-time cost regardless.
